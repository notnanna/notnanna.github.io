How to make a windmill fan



Materials:


  • Foam sheets
  • A pair of scissors
  • Glue stick
  • Ruler
  • A pen
  • Paper straws
  • Pipe cleaners

Steps:


  • 1. Cut up your sheet of foam into 12.5cm square

  • 2. Fold square diagonally in half (make a triangle). Unfold it, turn it arround and fold in the other direction

  • 3. Make a hole in the middle of square, where the creases meet. Cut halfway inwards, from each point
    and punch in holes are each corner - hole in the middle, hole on every other side

  • 4. Add some glue under each hole. bring one section into the midde (make sure the hole on the corner
    meets the hole in the middle) and stick it down. Keep folding each section with a hole down into the centre
    until there are no more holes left

  • 5. Using the lid of a gluestick and some leftover pieces of foam, mark the foam with and cut out the shape
    to make a circle. Put a hole in the middle (make two)

  • 6. Wrap pipecleaner around straw, put circle onto the pipecleaner, feed the pipecleaner through the hole in the
    windmill (from the back , through the front). put the second circle onto the front of the windmil

  • 7. Using a pair of scissors, cut a little bit of the pipecleaner off from the front, and twit the remainer into a little nob in the front

  • 8. Try out the windmill, and try spinning it a few times as the foam may be a bit still, so you will have to
    tweak it and find out wher it sits comfortably
